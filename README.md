# MycoStrain Explorer: Professional Mycological Intelligence

## Overview
MycoStrain Explorer is a high-fidelity phylogenetic analysis platform designed to bridge the gap between raw genomic data and field-ready mycological intelligence. By leveraging advanced LLM-driven synthesis and real-time grounding, the application provides researchers with a comprehensive view of fungal strain evolution, geographic distribution, and functional characteristics.

## Purpose
The primary mission of MycoStrain Explorer is to accelerate the identification and characterization of fungal haplotypes. It serves as a "Mission Control" for mycologists, offering a centralized interface to explore the complex evolutionary history of species ranging from common forest decomposers to rare medicinal and psychoactive strains.

## Key Benefits for Mycologists

### 1. Phylogenetic Haplotype Mapping
Visualize the geographic distribution of specific haplotypes across diverse ecological regions. The **Genomic Distribution Map** tracks regional prevalence, chemical markers, and ecological associations (e.g., insect relationships), allowing for precise spatial analysis of genetic diversity.

### 2. Evolutionary Timeline Reconstruction
Trace the divergence of strains through geological time. The **Evolutionary Timeline** provides estimates of divergence (in Millions of Years Ago) and correlates genetic shifts with major environmental or anthropogenic events.

### 3. Network Analysis & Genomic Connectivity
Explore the relationships between different haplotypes through an interactive **Genomic Connectivity Network**. Identify ancestral nodes, sister clades, and potential hybridization zones based on SNP (Single Nucleotide Polymorphism) markers.

### 4. Data Trust & Veracity Profiles
In an era of probabilistic data, MycoStrain Explorer introduces the **Data Trust Profile**. Every analysis includes a deterministic-to-probabilistic ratio, allowing researchers to distinguish between established genomic facts and synthesized phylogenetic hypotheses.

### 5. Dual-Mode Interface
- **Mycologist Mode**: Access deep technical data, including SNP registries, nucleotide diversity (π) metrics, and peer-reviewed grounding sources.
- **Enthusiast Mode**: A simplified interface focused on nature heritage, citizen science missions, and approachable species dossiers.

## Security & Integrity
MycoStrain Explorer utilizes a **Bio-Kernel Locked** security model. All API interactions are handled client-side; your Gemini API keys are stored strictly in your browser's local storage and are never transmitted to our servers, ensuring research privacy and data sovereignty.
